ARcademy

ARcademy is an augmented reality platform by Immersive Labs that builds educational AR experiences. We believe learning should be fun. That's why we use augmented reality technology to build imaginative educational apps that bring subjects to life for children. Founded in 2023 by a team of educators, game developers, and AR experts, we are on a mission to revolutionize the classroom experience with cutting-edge extended reality.

The ARcademy Difference

Augmented reality allows us to build enthralling environments where accurate models, objects, and information can exist alongside imaginative characters and narratives that are designed to resonate with children. This means we can teach subjects ranging from history to biology in a style that feels like an adventure game rather than a lecture. Our apps transform classrooms into environments where students actively participate, explore areas related to the lessons, and achieve knowledge goals similar to levels in a video game. The result is learning that feels rewarding rather than like a chore.

Our Vision

ARcademy's goal is to establish extended reality and gamification as key pillars of the classroom experience. We want to move education away from stale lectures and towards platforms that inspire kids to learn how the world works.
