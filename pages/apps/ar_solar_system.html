Blast Off With Your Kids: Exploring the Solar System with AR!

Remember learning about the solar system as a kid? Flat pictures in textbooks and dry lectures often failed to ignite real curiosity about the vastness of space. But what if your living room could become a launchpad for an interplanetary adventure? That's exactly what the "AR Solar System" app does!

The AR Solar System cleverly gamifies astronomy concepts that previously seemed daunting from textbooks alone. With the AR Solar System app, budding scientists embark on an interactive quest to study our sun and neighboring planets like never before. After scanning a images of the solar system with their device's camera, students can witness 3D planetary models materialize before their eyes, suspended in the air around them!
