OUR VISION

We envision a future where augmented reality enables solutions previously unimaginable across sectors like medicine, engineering, architecture, training & simulations, interactive spaces and many more. Our goal at ARcademy is to bring this future closer through cutting-edge products and strategic consulting.


OUR PLATFORMS & CONSULTING

Platform Development

ARcademy builds white-label AR apps for education, simulations, training, guidance & more. We leverage capabilities like 3D asset integration, multi-user networking, computer vision and spatial anchoring to bring augmented worlds to life tailored to your needs.


Consulting Services

Our consulting practice helps clients navigate the exponentially growing landscape of augmented reality applications across mobile, wearable, spatial computing and heads-up display technologies. We offer services like technology evaluation, use case discovery, requirements analysis, solution architecture, roadmapping and pilot preparation for AR initiatives. With development and consulting, ARcademy seeks to unlock transformative business outcomes using extended reality. Let us help you bridge the gap between the present and future with AR done right!
